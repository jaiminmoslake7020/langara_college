About Us

Simple split is a web application that helps people add their cost between friends and groups and give you a report based on spendings made by each person and options chosen by person to give the division information of cost. It will also help a person manage their cost and give you a report on how a person has made expenses in different categories and reports give an idea to a person where they can save money.


Simple split web application wants to help people in keeping the records of expenses they make not only for them but also for other people. So they can know how much they owe to X person and how much Y person owes to them. This way they will have a clear idea about how much money they have and can spend according to it. Additionally, Simple Split will give report category wise where they have spent most of the money so in future they can take care of the extra spendings.


Let see how it can help our ideal user Jim Harper.

Jim Harper is a 26-year-old male who is living in Vancouver. He right now livers at 33rd avenue Fraser street with 3 other people in sharing. They all do grocery shopping together or whenever a person from the group is free goes for grocery shopping and shops for different things what they need so at different times different person makes expenses and it is hard to remember all expenses. So here Jim can use our app and solve all these problems of keeping records of all these expenses. Because Jim leaves in sharing and shares his expenses with other people, this situation makes him the ideal customer of our web app.
