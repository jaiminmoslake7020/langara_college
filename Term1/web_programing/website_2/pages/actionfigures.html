Gears of War

It's time to hunt down those Locust and eradicate them once and for all! From Epic Games' finale in the Gears of War video game series, these 7-inch Gears of War 3 action figures come with plenty of accessories and articulation. The amazing articulation includes ab crunch, pectoral movement, double elbows, and much, much more. Plus, additional weapons can peg to the figures' backs!
