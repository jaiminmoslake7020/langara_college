Flying Origami Heart

The Flying Origami Heart designer is Paul Ee. The paper size used for this is 5/7/10 cm and the paper ratio is square. Each of these cute little origami hearts is made from a single sheet of red-white paper, without any cuts or glue.

Horse

The horse origami is made from two coloured sheets brown and grey paper. No usage of glue or cuts is done. It is made by the fold procedure of the paper. The paper size used for this is 7/8/10cm.

Owl

The owl origami is difficult to make as not just the fold procedure will work one have to be creative enough to make this origami. The two colouredsheets which are used in this origami is brown and white. This great origami is made without any fevicol or cuts.

Paper-Mache

Paper mache is one of those crafts that is easy enough for children but can be sophisticated in the hands of an artistic adult. It is also an inexpensive project because most of what you need for it is recycled or found in your kitchen. The material required is newspaper, flour, pot, balloon, paint colours, whisk and scissors.

RHINO

The origami model was made in the program 3D Max 2017 Units setup -Centimeters The grid of the model was optimized by the Proptimizer modifier. The archive contains a texture Normal and Bump.

Rainbow Brolly

For making rainbow brolly seven different coloured paper sheets are used. Many other materials are also used for making this origami. This craft does not involve the usage of scissor or glue, the fold procedure is used to make the umbrella. The paper size used for this is 7/8/10cm.
