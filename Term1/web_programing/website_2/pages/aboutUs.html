WHO WE ARE

Hobbies Collectibles is located in different countries but the Hobbies Collectibles established in Abbotsford is the oldest one and is considered as the heart of BC's Fraser Valley. Since 1992, we have been serving collectors and hobbyists, both young and old generation people like it. Approximately 50 mintues from Vancouver, we have the largest selection of sports collectibles in the Lower Mainland and it is opened recently only few months back. We carry the hottest new collectible card games (CCG's), plush toys, stamps and coins supplies to fill your collectible needs. Keeping in mind the creative aspect Origami and Antiques are also available in all Hobbies Collectibles stores. With over 40 years of retail experience, owners John & Darren enjoy in finding the unique collectibles for you.
